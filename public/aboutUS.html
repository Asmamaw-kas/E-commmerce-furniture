About Us

Welcome to SABA Furniture – Where Comfort Meets Style

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to offer beautifully crafted furniture that combines functionality, durability, and timeless design. We strive to make your shopping experience seamless, from browsing our collections to enjoying your new furniture in your home.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by ABEBAW KASSIE, SABA Furniture started as a small workshop with a big dream. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in the furniture industry, serving thousands of happy customers across ETHIOPIA. Our passion for design and commitment to quality remain at the heart of everything we do.

Why Choose Us?

  • Quality Craftsmanship: Every piece of furniture is designed with care and built to last.
  • Wide Selection: From modern minimalism to classic elegance, we offer a diverse range of styles to suit every taste.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: Your satisfaction is our priority. Our friendly team is always here to assist you.
  • Sustainable Practices: We are committed to eco-friendly materials and responsible manufacturing processes.

Our Promise

When you shop with SABA Furniture, you’re not just buying furniture – you’re investing in a better living experience. We stand behind our products with 2017 warranty and a hassle-free return policy.
